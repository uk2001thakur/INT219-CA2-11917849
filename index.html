LOvely Professional University

This post is written by Ashok Mittal

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university situated in Phagwara, Punjab, India. The university was established in 2005[2] by Lovely International Trust, under The Lovely Professional University Act, 2005 (Punjab Act 25 of 2005)[3] and started operation in 2006. The university campus spreads over 600 acres (2.4 km2) in boundary on the outskirts of Jalandhar and houses 10,000 students (as of 2010).
